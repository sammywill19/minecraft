Complete Minecraft 1.21 Guide

A comprehensive guide to Minecraft, covering everything from starting the game to exploring structures like Bastions, Mineshafts, Nether Fortresses, End Cities, and more, plus what to do after beating the game.

Step 1: Starting Your World

  1. Create a new world in "Survival" mode.
  2. Gather wood by punching trees and craft a Crafting Table (4 Planks → Crafting Table).
  3. Craft basic wooden tools:
    • Wooden Pickaxe: 3 Planks + 2 Sticks
    • Wooden Axe: 3 Planks + 2 Sticks
  4. Build a small shelter using dirt, wood, or stone to survive the first night.
  5. Craft a Bed (3 Wool + 3 Planks) to skip the night if you find sheep.

Step 2: Early Game Progression

Food and Farming

  1. Kill animals like cows, pigs, and chickens for food.
  2. Craft a Hoe (2 Planks + 2 Sticks) and start farming by planting seeds.

Mining Resources

  1. Find a cave or dig down to collect:
    • Coal: For torches and smelting.
    • Iron Ore: Smelt into ingots for stronger tools and armor.
  2. Craft essential items:
    • Furnace: 8 Cobblestone → Furnace
    • Torches: 1 Stick + 1 Coal → 4 Torches
  3. Craft and equip iron tools and armor.

Step 3: Advanced Mining

  1. Mine down to Y-level -59 to find diamonds.
  2. Craft a Diamond Pickaxe (3 Diamonds + 2 Sticks) to mine obsidian.
  3. Mine obsidian to:
    • Build a Nether Portal (10 Obsidian).
    • Craft an Enchantment Table (4 Obsidian + 2 Diamonds + 1 Book).
  4. Set up an enchantment area with bookshelves (3 Books + 6 Planks → Bookshelf).

Step 4: Enchantments

  1. Use an Enchantment Table to enchant your gear:
    • Basic enchantments: Protection, Sharpness, Efficiency, and Unbreaking.
    • Use Lapis Lazuli and XP levels to enchant.

Step 5: The Nether

  1. Build a Nether Portal and enter the Nether.
  2. Prepare for dangerous mobs like Ghasts, Piglins, and Magma Cubes.
  3. Mine resources like:
    • Nether Quartz: For XP and Redstone builds.
    • Gold Nuggets: Found in Nether gold ore.

Nether Fortresses

Nether Fortresses are large structures in the Nether made of Nether bricks. They are essential for game progression.

Why Explore Nether Fortresses?

  • Farm Blaze Rods, which are required to craft Eyes of Ender.
  • Find loot chests containing diamonds, gold, and saddles.
  • Wither Skeletons spawn here, dropping Wither Skeleton Skulls for summoning the Wither Boss.

How to Prepare:

  • Wear armor enchanted with Fire Protection to resist fire and lava.
  • Bring a bow and arrows to fight Blazes from a distance.
  • Carry potions like Fire Resistance and Healing.

Step 6: Netherite Mining

What is Netherite?

Netherite is a powerful material used to upgrade diamond gear. It’s crafted from Ancient Debris, found in the Nether.

How to Find Netherite

  1. Go to Y-level 15 in the Nether.
  2. Use one of these methods to mine:
    • Bed Explosions: Beds explode in the Nether. Place a bed, shield yourself with blocks, and right-click to blow up large areas.
    • TNT Mining: Craft TNT (4 Sand + 5 Gunpowder) to create controlled explosions.
    • Strip Mining: Dig long tunnels and mine manually.
  3. Smelt Ancient Debris into Netherite Scraps.
  4. Combine 4 Netherite Scraps + 4 Gold Ingots to craft a Netherite Ingot.
  5. Use a Smithing Table (2 Planks + 2 Iron Ingots) to upgrade diamond gear to Netherite gear.

Step 7: Preparing for the End

  1. Craft Eyes of Ender:
    • Blaze Powder + Ender Pearls → Eyes of Ender
  2. Throw Eyes of Ender to locate the Stronghold.
  3. Activate the End Portal by placing Eyes of Ender in the portal frame.

Step 8: Defeating the Ender Dragon

  1. Destroy the End Crystals to stop the dragon from healing.
  2. Attack the dragon with a bow or sword.
  3. Use beds to deal massive damage when the dragon is perched.
  4. Defeat the dragon and collect the Dragon Egg as a trophy.

End Cities

End Cities are structures in the End dimension that contain some of the best loot in the game. They are found on the outer End islands after defeating the Ender Dragon.

Why Explore End Cities?

  • Find Elytra in End Ships, which allow you to glide through the air.
  • Loot enchanted gear, including armor, tools, and weapons.
  • Collect Shulker Shells to craft Shulker Boxes (portable storage).

How to Prepare:

  • Bring Ender Pearls to teleport across large gaps.
  • Use a Potion of Slow Falling to survive Shulker attacks.
  • Carry plenty of food and strong armor.

What to Do After Beating Minecraft

Beating the Ender Dragon is just the beginning! Here are some of the best things to do after completing the main game:

1. Explore and Conquer

  • Find and loot more End Cities for Elytra and enchanted gear.
  • Explore Bastions in the Nether for gold blocks and rare items like the Pigstep music disc.
  • Search for Woodland Mansions in the Overworld for rare loot and combat challenges.

2. Build Ambitious Projects

  • Construct a massive base, castle, or city.
  • Build an automatic sorting system for your items using Redstone and Hoppers.
  • Create a themed build, like a medieval village or futuristic space station.

3. Automate Farms and Systems

  • Build an Iron Farm to produce unlimited iron.
  • Automate crop farms for wheat, carrots, potatoes, and melons.
  • Create mob farms for XP, rare drops, and materials like gunpowder and bones.

4. Fight the Wither Boss

  • Collect Wither Skeleton Skulls from Nether Fortresses.
  • Summon and defeat the Wither Boss to obtain a Nether Star.
  • Use the Nether Star to craft a Beacon for powerful buffs.

5. Master Redstone

  • Learn advanced Redstone mechanics to create automatic doors, traps, and machines.
  • Build a fully functional elevator or rollercoaster.
  • Create a secret base with hidden entrances and Redstone contraptions.

6. Complete Achievements

  • Work on completing all advancements in the game, such as:
    • “How Did We Get Here?”: Obtain every status effect simultaneously.
    • “Beaconator”: Create a fully powered Beacon.

7. Explore Rare Biomes

  • Find a Mushroom Island biome and build a base there.
  • Explore the Deep Dark biome and challenge the Warden.
  • Discover a Frozen Ocean biome and collect Blue Ice.

8. Multiplayer Adventures

  • Join a multiplayer server and collaborate with others on massive projects.
  • Challenge friends to PvP battles or mini-games.
  • Trade with other players to gather rare items.
